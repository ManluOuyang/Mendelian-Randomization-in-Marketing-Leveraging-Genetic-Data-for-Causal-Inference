# Data Availability

The raw data used in this study cannot be redistributed due to licensing restrictions. Here we provide references to the original studies and instructions for accessing the corresponding GWAS summary statistics.

---

**Cannabis Use Disorder**:
Levey, Daniel F., et al. "Multi-ancestry genome-wide association study of cannabis use disorder yields insight into disease biology and public health implications." Nature Genetics 55.12 (2023): 2094-2103.

According to the reporting summary of Levey et al (2023), the GWAS summary statistics generated during and/or analyzed during the current study will be available via dbGAP; the dbGaP accession assigned to the Million Veteran Program is phs001672.v1.p. The website is: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/projects/gap/cgi-bin/study.cgi?study_id=phs001672.v1.p1.

---

**Educational Attainment**: 
Okbay, Aysu, et al. "Polygenic prediction of educational attainment within and between families from genome-wide association analyses in 3 million individuals." Nature genetics 54.4 (2022): 437-449.

According to the paper, GWAS summary statistics can be downloaded from http://www.thessgac.org/data subject to a terms of use to encourage responsible use of the data.
